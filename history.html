History

Established in 2010, QVC(Quality Value Concept) Engineering and construction is a Singapore-based company providing complete building envelope solutions. We take an integrated approach to provide design, build and supply solutions across Asia. Whether it’s unique structures, restoration or renovation projects, or curtain walls, our flexibility and experience enable us to anticipate challenges, find innovative solutions and deliver complete building envelope solutions with pride. Whatever the project challenge, QVC takes an integrated approach to deliver the building envelope solution that meets your goal. Presently, QVC main areas of business for building envelopes are as follows: Building Envelopes Customised Structures and Art Structures Security Façade Interior Fit-Outs

Quality

Quality objectives are the SMART goals for enhancing customer satisfaction and are relevant to the quality policy of the company. Quality objectives are defined when establishing the quality management system or during management review. It is also called a Key performance indicator. KPI shall be defined by each department.



Value

Engineering and construction bring value by providing innovative and sustainable solutions for building and infrastructure projects, optimizing resources, reducing risks, ensuring quality and safety, and delivering projects on time and within budget, to meet the needs and expectations of clients and stakeholders.



Concept

A company's business concept is about the direction and form of its operations. If it is apparent to you that the company's success lies in its intangible assets, think carefully about protecting them at an early stage. A business concept is a fundamental prerequisite for the existence of a company.
